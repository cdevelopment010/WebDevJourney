Weight area

Our weight training area is suitable for everyone, with a wide range of machines, free weights and mats for body weight exercies. If you don't know where to start, then please ask our helpful staff for advice, or talk to one of our personal trainers.

Our range of weights include, but not limited to:

  • Incline, decline and flat bench presses
  • Squat racks
  • Pullup and dip stations
  • Cable fly machines
  • Leg Press
  • Racks of dumbbells
  • Deadlift stations
  • Area for body weight training
  • Ab rollers
  • Resistance bands
  • Foam rollers
